Palindrome Checker

A palindrome is a word, phrase, number, or sequence of characters that reads the same forward and backward, ignoring spaces, punctuation, and capitalization.


For example:


  • "racecar" → same forward and backward.

  • "A man, a plan, a canal. Panama" → when spaces, punctuation, and capitalization are removed, it becomes "amanaplanacanalpanama", which is the same in reverse.
